Area information

The informal sector has the highest number of businesses, (150,138), accounting for more than 87% of total number of businesses. There are 10,569 businesses employing 5 or more persons,9, 13% of total businesses.

It is not surprising that the Central Region has the highest number of formal businesses and, although Western Region is second to Central Region, it has only 16 % of formal businesses, while Eastern and Northern Regions have 14% and 7% of formal businesses respectively.
